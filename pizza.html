Mozarella Pizza

Fresh Mozarella Margerita Pizza

The creamy texture of the melted Fresh Mozzarella, the succulent taste of the warm tomatoes, and the rich tomato sauce, blend perfectly to make this pizza an instant favorite.

Ingredient list

  • 8 oz. Galbani Fresh Mozarella Ball, Sliced
  • 1 thin pizza crust
  • 3 Tbsp. extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 tomatoes, sliced
  • 1 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1/2 Tbsp. dried oregano

Cooking directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
  2. Lightly brush pizza crust with olive oil. Spread pizza sauce evenly on crust
  3. Decorate pizza with fresh mozarella and tomato slices, leaving just a one half inch rim of crust exposed around pizza. Sprinkle the fresh basil and dried oregano across the pizza.
  4. Bake pizza in oven for about 14-16 minutes or until crust is finished and pizza is hot.
  5. Let stand 3-4 minutes before cutting
  6. Add crushed red pepper to taste
