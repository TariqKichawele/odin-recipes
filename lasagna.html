Meatball Lasagna

This is the best classic lasagna recipe made with parmesan ricotta cheese filling, melted mozarella cheese, lasagna noodles, and a robust tomato meat sauce. It is the best lasagna recipe.

Ingredient list

  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozarella chesse
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Robust tomato meat sauce

Steps

  1. Start by making a homemade meat sauce. This can be done by sauteing an onion in olive oil until it begins to soften. This brings out the sweet flavor of the onion. Then it is time to brown the ground beef until it is browned and the onion is softened. Stir in the marinara sauce and let it simmer on low.
  2. Make a parmesan ricotta cheese mixture.This is what gives the lasagna the creamy filling. Its best to flavor the ricotta chesse with a good amount of parmesan cheese, spices and egg to bind it together.
  3. Use your favorite lasgana noodles. There are two choices for lasagna noodles oven ready pre-cooked noodles ot lasagna noodles that need to be cooked before hitting the oven. I have tried both ways and can attest that they both work.
  4. Bring on the cheese! The best lasagna is full of cheese so be generous with the layers of mozarella cheese
  5. Bake covered for 15 minutes and then remove the foil and continue to cook until the cheese is bubbly. Let it set up for 10 minutes before serving.
